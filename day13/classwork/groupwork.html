poodle

poodles Poodles are intelligent and versatile dogs known for their curly, hypoallergenic coats. Available in standard, miniature, and toy sizes, they excel in training and various dog sports. german Shepherd

german Shepherds are intelligent, loyal, and versatile dogs, originally bred for herding in Germany. Known for their strength and striking black-and-tan coats, they excel in roles like police work and service assistance. Their protective instincts and gentle nature with families make them excellent companions, requiring regular exercise and socialization.
