guinea pigs

guinea pig

Guinea pigs, or cavies, are small, sociable rodents native to the Andes in South America. They are popular pets due to their friendly nature and gentle disposition. Typically weighing between 1.5 to 2.5 pounds, guinea pigs come in various breeds, each with unique fur types and colors. They require a spacious habitat, a balanced diet rich in hay, fresh vegetables, and pellets, and social interaction, either with humans or other guinea pigs. Known for their vocalizations, they communicate through chirps, whistles, and purrs. With proper care, guinea pigs can live 5 to 7 years, providing companionship and joy to their owners.
