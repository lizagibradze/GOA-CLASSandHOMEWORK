about f1

Formula 1, often abbreviated as F1, is the pinnacle of motor racing and is governed by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). It features a series of races known as Grands Prix, held on various circuits around the world. Teams compete with highly engineered, aerodynamic cars powered by hybrid engines, combining performance and efficiency. The championship includes two titles: the Drivers' Championship and the Constructors' Championship. Renowned for its speed, technology, and glamour, F1 attracts millions of fans globally. Iconic teams like Ferrari and Mercedes dominate the sport, while legendary drivers like Lewis Hamilton and Michael Schumacher have left indelible marks on its history.
